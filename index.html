GRAND SEASONS - THE BUSINESS HOTEL

Enjoy a Luxury Experience

Grand Seasons is a business hotel located off Outer Ring Road, it is 9 minutes away from Krishnarajapuram Railway Station, 10 minutes away from Baiyappanahalli Metro Station, and about an hour away from Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru. The well-connected Grand Seasons Hotel is easy to commute for all, regardless of what their choice of transport is. The resplendent banquet halls available at Grand Seasons Hotel, Bangalore, are of varying capacities and make for an awesome place for you to host weddings, receptions, sangeet, mehndi, haldi, and engagement ceremonies, birthday parties, wedding anniversaries, reunions, kitty parties, promotion parties, farewell parties, board meetings, corporate seminars, conferences, exhibitions, office parties, orientations, training programs, team building activities, and much more. Rated with 4 stars, this high-quality property provides guests with access to massage, restaurant and fitness center on-site.

Banquet Halls

Grand Seasons Hotel has 2 beautiful banquet halls of capacity upto 300 people can manage in floating. All venues has centralised ac, attached dress changing rooms and separate dining area. Outside food / catering is not allowed, inhouse catering facility is available and guest need to pay per plate system. Banquet Hall provides good number of facilities to organize an event. The banquet hall provides room facility for the guests. The Banquet Hall facility serves non vegetarian food and liquor. Grand Seasons Hotel has spacious car parking which can park upto 50 cars. Valet parking is available for the guest's vehicles to have hassle free parking. DJ party can be organized in the venue. Grand Seasons Hotel is a perfect place for any family or corporate event. Events permitted at Grand Seasons Hotel are Engagement, Wedding, Reception, Sangeet, Mehandi, Baby Shower, Wedding Anniversary, Bachelor Party, Baptism, Birthday Party, Get-Together, Kitty Party, Naming Ceremony, Ring Ceremony, Freshers Party, Upanayanam . We promise a Professional service with a smile.
